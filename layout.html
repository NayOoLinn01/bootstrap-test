LUX

[HOTFIX] Since her rework, Lux has been over performing in the Mid and Baron Lane. To give her foes more possibi-light-ies to counter her, we’re making adjustments to her passive and second ability. These changes will also make Lux choose between protecting herself or protecting her teammates.

ILLUMINATION

  • Damage: 32 + Level × 8 + 25% Ability Power → 25 + Level × 7 + 25% Ability Power

PRISMATIC BARRIER

  • Cooldown: 11/10/9/8s → 12/11/10/9s
  • Shield gained from first cast: 40/75/110/145 + 10% Ability Power → 30/55/80/105 + 15% Ability Power
  • Shield gained from second cast: 80/150/220/290 + 20% Ability Power → 48/88/128/168 + 24% Ability Power
